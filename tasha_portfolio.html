Tasha's Portfolio

I wanted to design my own portfolio to explore and learn more about HTML, CSS, and specifically JavaScript. Whenever I engage in creating a new project, I always want to learn new skills or methods to improve and challenge myself.

The idea behind the theme of this website is the moon. To me, I find the moon inspiring and it fits my own personality. I consider myself a calm and reflective person, so the moon was a good concept to start with when designing this portfolio.

I am also a very creative person, so I had the idea of making a different design for each page of my projects. I got this inspiration from a certain website about video games I like. One element that I loved is that I felt like I could “interact” with the medium, instead of just navigating through. It put a lot more importance on the projects I was looking at, making it more fun to navigate.

It isn’t the first design I have made. I decided to improve it to become a bit more interactive and simple. My first design was based on the fact I was a Cinema student, and wanted to go with a sort of “modern” website, while still choosing colors outside of white and black. I prefer this design because it represents, to me, serenity and calm. The colors are a good mix of the two things I am passionate about: art and technology.

In terms of improvement, I wish I could make something even more complex. There are also some part, like in the main page, where scrolling down isn't as smooth. I definitely want to learn more about how to make a smoother interactive website.
