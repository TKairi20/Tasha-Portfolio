About Me

My name is Tasha Pakita Kanzi, a current student in the Certificate of Arts and Science at Concordia University, previous Cinema & Communications student at Dawson College. I have a lot of passion for creative projects using technology.


I am interested in exploring digital mediums like interactive arts, web designing and video games. My goal is to become a creative developer who can design original platforms and interactive projects that mixes both art and technology.
